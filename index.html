About Anna M. Mason

Experience.
Dedication.

Anna Mason began her legal career as a paralegal in 1993 and spent more than 20 years building deep expertise before earning her Juris Doctorate from Capital University Law School in 2020 — graduating Cum Laude from Ohio University along the way.

She is the current President of the Athens County Bar Association and serves on multiple Ohio State Bar Association councils. She also holds an Ohio title insurance agent license and founded Foundation Title Agency, LLC in 2021.
