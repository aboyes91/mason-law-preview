About Foundation Title Agency

One-Stop Real Estate
Closing Services

Foundation Title Agency, LLC is a full-service title company offering real estate closing services for both residential and commercial transactions. Conveniently located alongside Mason Law Office in Nelsonville, Ohio, we provide seamless, coordinated service for buyers, sellers, lenders, and real estate professionals throughout the region.

Our experienced team manages every detail of the closing process — from the initial title search and examination through settlement and final recording — ensuring your transaction closes smoothly and on time. Having a licensed attorney and experienced title team under one roof means you get integrated legal and title services without the hassle of coordinating between multiple offices.

The Closing Process

What to Expect

01
Title Search & Examination

We conduct a thorough search of public records to identify any liens, encumbrances, or title defects that could affect ownership — and review the results to ensure a clear title.

02
Title Insurance Commitment

We issue a title insurance commitment outlining the conditions for issuing a final policy, protecting the buyer and lender from covered title risks.

03
Settlement & Closing

We prepare all closing documents, coordinate signatures, and facilitate the transfer of funds between all parties — ensuring a smooth and accurate closing day.

04
Recording & Policy Issuance

After closing, we record all documents with the appropriate county recorder and issue the final title insurance policies to the owner and lender.
