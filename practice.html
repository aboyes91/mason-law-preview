Areas of Law

What We Practice

📜

Probate Law

We guide families through the probate process with care and precision — from filing to final distribution — so you can focus on what truly matters during a difficult time. We handle both testate and intestate estates and work to keep the process as smooth and efficient as possible.

🏛️

Estate Planning

Thoughtful estate plans protect your loved ones and ensure your wishes are honored. We craft wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives tailored to your life and goals. No templates — every plan is built around your family, your assets, and what matters most to you.

🏠

Real Estate

From residential purchases to commercial transactions, we provide sound legal counsel at every stage of the real estate process. We also offer full closing services through our sister company, Foundation Title Agency, LLC — giving you seamless, one-stop support.

🔑

Landlord / Tenant Law

We represent both landlords and tenants in disputes, lease reviews, eviction proceedings, and other matters governed by Ohio landlord-tenant law. Whether you own rental property or are navigating a difficult tenancy, we help you understand your rights and take the right steps.

💼

Corporate Law

Whether you're forming a new entity, drafting operating agreements, or navigating a business transaction, we provide practical legal support for Ohio businesses of all sizes. We help you build a solid legal foundation so you can focus on growing your business.

⚖️

Civil Litigation

When disputes require courtroom resolution, we advocate assertively for our clients while always pursuing the most efficient and cost-effective path to a favorable outcome. We handle a broad range of civil matters in Ohio state courts.

About the Firm

Meet the Team

Mason Law Office, LLC is a full-service law firm located in Nelsonville, Ohio, serving clients throughout Athens, Hocking, and surrounding counties. We combine deep legal expertise with genuine personal attention — because we believe every client deserves a lawyer who truly listens.

Anna M. Mason

Attorney — Mason Law Office, LLC

Anna Mason began her legal career as a paralegal, receiving her paralegal certificate from the Tri-County Adult Career Center in 1993. After more than 20 years as a paralegal, she decided to further her career by becoming an attorney. Anna graduated Cum Laude from Ohio University in 2016 and received her Juris Doctorate from Capital University Law School in 2020. She also earned her license as an Ohio title insurance agent in July of 2020 and opened Foundation Title Agency, LLC the following year.

During her studies at Capital Law, Anna was recognized for outstanding achievement in civil pretrial proceedings and received the Capital Law School Ambassador Award. She also served as an Ohio Supreme Court qualified Guardian Ad Litem from 2009 through 2014.

Anna is the current President of the Athens County Bar Association and Treasurer for the Hocking County Bar Association. She serves on the Council of Delegates and the Legislative Drafting Committee of the Ohio State Bar Association, and is a member of the Real Estate Section Council and Solo & Small Firms General Practice Section Council. She resides in Logan and enjoys spending time with her family, especially her grandchildren.

Tracie Newlun

Legal Assistant — Mason Law Office & Foundation Title Agency

Tracie Newlun was born in Nelsonville, Ohio and attended Logan High School, graduating in 1985. She went on to work in the real estate department of the Hocking County Auditor's Office from July 1985 to August 1991, then accepted a position with the real estate department in the Athens County Auditor's Office, where she worked until she retired in November 2018.

In January 2022, Tracie decided to come out of retirement and currently works as a legal assistant to Attorney Anna M. Mason at Mason Law Office and Foundation Title Agency in Nelsonville, Ohio. She brings decades of real estate administrative expertise that benefits our clients every day.

Tracie married her high school sweetheart, Bill, in 1986, and they made their home in Nelsonville where they raised their two children, Rachel and Ryan. She enjoys spending time with her family, especially her granddaughter.
